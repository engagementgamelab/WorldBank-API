======================
WORLD BANK RBF HOW-TO:
======================

Please ensure you utilize the following structure for files.
----------
Phase One:
|
content
	|
	phase_one
		|
		city (e.g. malcolm)
		|
		dialogue_file.yml (where dialogue_file is the symbol for the file)

Phase Two:
|
content
	|
	phase_two
		|
		problem_file.yml (where problem_file is the symbol for the file)
----------

Resources:

Validate your YAML syntax at yamllint.com.

YAML syntax help at http://docs.ansible.com/YAMLSyntax.html.
